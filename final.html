About VN Logistics

With over 15 years of work experience in the freight forwarding industry, Established VN Logistics. VN Logistics has established itself as a trusted partner for businesses seeking reliable international shipping solutions. We specialize in air, ocean, and land transportation, offering comprehensive logistics services tailored to your needs.

Our commitment to excellence, combined with our global network and advanced tracking technology, ensures your cargo arrives safely and on time, every time.

Our Services

Air Freight

Fast and reliable air cargo services for time-sensitive shipments. Global coverage with competitive rates and real-time tracking.

Ocean Freight

Cost-effective sea freight solutions for large-volume shipments. Full container load (FCL) and less than container load (LCL) options available.

Land Transport

Efficient ground transportation and last-mile delivery services. Door-to-door solutions for seamless logistics operations.

Door to Door Service

Complete end-to-end logistics solutions from pickup at origin to delivery at destination, ensuring a smooth and hassle-free shipping experience.

Trading

Import and export trading services with reliable sourcing, documentation handling, and compliance support for international markets.
